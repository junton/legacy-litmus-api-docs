From an API perspective, mobile results looked identical to email results. They have inbox and email image urls that you can use to display the results to your users.

 

However the email view will be a taller, stitched together image that's not suitable to return to your users as a single, long image. Instead, you'll need to wrap the image in the appropriate client-side code.

 

See Integrating our mobile email clients for more information and links to sample code.
