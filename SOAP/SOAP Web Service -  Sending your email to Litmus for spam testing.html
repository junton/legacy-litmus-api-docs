So now you've created a test and sent an email to Litmus to run the test.  If you are running a spam test, that is including spam clients in your results, then you'll want to perform one additional task.   When determining if your email is spam services test two key components: the content of your email and your reputation.  In order to test your reputation you need to send an email directly into the spam service.  So we'll need to do just that.

 

This sample code will fetch Litmus' most update list of spam seed addresses:

 

var addresses =LitmusApi.GetSpamSeedAddresses("api-key", "api-pass");

 

This will return an array of email addresses for you to send your email to.  It is very important when performing a spam test to send your email to each of these addresses.
